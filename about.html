My story

Living in NYC (with my amazing partner, David, and our two rescue cats, Jester & Jynx), I am training at HB Studios, Upright Citizen's Brigade and The People's Improv Theater. I train all around town with various coaches. And I'm currently producing my own films. My google calendar is color coded and very busy, and I love it!

I graduated from the University of Nevada, Las Vegas with a BA in Stage and Screen Acting. In Las Vegas, I completed courses in improvisation and clown at Vegas Theater Hub. I also trained in Mixed Martial Arts, did burlesque, and stand-up.
